Who Were the Signers

Famous Signers

Some of the most famous and widely recognized signers of the Declaration of Independence include:

Thomas Jefferson
Primary author of the Declaration, future 3rd U.S. President.
John Adams
Major advocate for independence, future 2nd U.S. President.
Benjamin Franklin
Prominent inventor, diplomat, and elder statesman.
John Hancock
President of the Continental Congress, famous for his large signature.
Samuel Adams
Leader of the Sons of Liberty and key revolutionary organizer.
Roger Sherman
Only person to sign the Declaration, Articles of Confederation, and U.S. Constitution.
Charles Carroll of Carrollton
The only Catholic signer, very wealthy.
Richard Henry Lee
Proposed the resolution declaring independence.
George Wythe
Influential legal scholar and mentor to Thomas Jefferson.
