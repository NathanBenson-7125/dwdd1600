Who Are We?

Welcome to Fortnite Lore & Timeline — a website dedicated to compiling the entire story of Epic Games’ Fortnite in one easy-to-navigate place. Our goal is to make the game’s complex and ever-changing storyline simple to understand by organizing every major & minor event in chronological order, using Fortnite’s official system of "Chapters." Whether you’re a longtime player wanting to revisit past seasons, or a curious fan trying to piece together the lore, this site will guide you through the full history of the Fortnite universe, step by step.

To make exploring the lore even easier, each chapter is broken down into its key seasons, major events, and important characters. Instead of digging through live event gameplay on YouTube, questline dialogue, or scattered wikis, everything is gathered in one place with clear summaries and context. This site is designed for both casual fans who just want the basics and dedicated lore hunters who want the full picture.

Key Terms & Entities:

The Zero Point
The mysterious energy at the heart of the island, driving many events.
Rifts
A tear in reality that alters the island and brings in new elements from other dimensions.
The Cube / "Kevin"
A powerful floating cube that appeared in Chapter 1, Season 6 and caused major map changes across multiple maps.
